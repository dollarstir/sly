Welcome to Gt Kamot Trucks & Spare Parts

Anema Trucks has been a very well known company for over 80 years in the used trucks & spare parts business. We are one of the leading companies in the worldwide buying and selling of used trucks, tractor-units, semi-trailers, trailers and truck parts. We are well known as a reliable supplier on the import and export market of used trucks and spare parts, mostly for the extended service we can offer to our customers, like:

  • Taking care of all necessary (export) documents
  • Door to door transport
  • Loading of driven units and containers
  • The entire shipment

You find a constant stock of 300 trucks and trailers of all common brands and in every price range in our yard. If you require a truck or part which is not available we can supply it in short time. Please check our stock for the actual offers or contact us. We will be pleased to help you.
