Gt Kamot Trucks & Parts uses the following privacy policy with regard to the use of its website www.anema.nl .

General

Gt Kamot Trucks & Spare Parts never uses data about visitors to its websites to send offers of products or services (direct marketing, e-mail spam, etc.). Gt Kamot Trucks & Spare Parts does not make data about visitors to its websites available to third parties.

Where reference is made below to technical characteristics of a query, click, display, search query or the like, this means: requested url, IP number, time, HTTP referer url (sometimes including your search terms for a search engine), user agent string, browser type, screen size and the like. We do not check (if at all) who uses which IP number at what time and we do not trace this data back to your person.

Gt Kamot Trucks & Spare Parts does not register any special personal data, with regard to, for example, religion or belief, race or origin, health, sexual life, criminal law, political views or membership of a trade union.

Web visit

When you visit our websites, the technical characteristics of your requests are recorded in so-called log files on our servers. These data are kept for several months only for statistical analysis purposes. We do not check (if at all) who uses which IP number at what time and we do not trace this data back to your person.

Searches

When you use a search function on our websites, your search terms together with the technical characteristics can be stored in so-called log files on our servers. These data are kept for several months only for statistical analysis purposes. We do not check (if at all) who uses which IP number at what time and we do not trace this data back to your person.

Ad impressions and clicks

Advertising impressions and clicks are registered by Gt Kamot Trucks & Spare Parts and by advertising networks in which Gt Kamot Trucks & Parts participates.

When you click on an advertisement, the technical characteristics of the click can be stored by Gt Kamot Trucks & Spare Parts in so-called log files that are kept for several months for the purpose of financial reporting. We do not check (if at all) who uses which IP number at what time and we do not trace this data back to your person.

Gt Kamot Trucks & Spare Parts participates in the following advertising networks, which conduct their own registrations and have their own privacy policy (the hyperlinks lead to the privacy policy of the relevant party):

  • Google Adwords content network

When displaying advertisements and so-called 'web beacons', these parties can place so-called 'cookies' in and read from your web browser. A web beacon is a small invisible image that is integrated into a web page to register a request for that page. You can think of a cookie as a small file with impersonal data, which is used to associate different requests from your browser. A cookie can be used by Gt Kamot Trucks & Spare Parts and the advertising networks are used, for example, to store your preferences or user settings, to stay logged in to an interactive part of a website, or to tailor advertisements to the interests and preferences that are apparent from your click behavior.

With the default settings, most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually refuse or selectively accept cookies by adjusting the settings of your browser. If you disable cookies, certain functions of our websites may not be available (in particular: login mechanisms). On the following websites you will find information about commonly used browsers and how you can adjust the cookie settings in the respective browser:

  • Internet Explorer (Microsoft)
  • Firefox (Mozilla)
  • Safari (Apple)

Google Analytics

Gt Kamot Trucks & Spare Parts may use Google Analytics, a web analysis service provided by Google Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics uses cookies to Gt Kamot Trucks & Spare Parts help analyze how users use the site. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website (including your IP address) will be transferred to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Google uses this information to keep track of how you use the website, to compile reports on website activity for website operators and to offer other services related to website activity and internet usage. Google may provide this information to third parties if Google is legally obliged to do so, or insofar as these third parties process the information on behalf of Google. Google will not combine your IP address with other data that Google has. You can refuse the use of cookies as indicated above.

Newsletters

When you subscribe or unsubscribe from one of our e-mail newsletters, the technical characteristics of your mutation will be stored together with your e-mail address for several months. In addition, your e-mail address will be kept in the subscriber list for the entire duration of your subscription only for the purpose of sending the relevant newsletter. You are free to use an e-mail address for your subscription that cannot be traced back to you. You can unsubscribe from any of our newsletters at any time, in a reasonable and straightforward manner which is stated at the bottom of each edition of the relevant newsletter. If you are unable to unsubscribe in this way, we will exceptionally remove you from the subscriber list upon receipt of a written request to that effect.

Contact forms and email

When you fill in and send us a contact, registration or information form on one of our websites, or send us an e-mail, the data you send us together with the technical characteristics will be kept by us for as long as the nature of the form and your message is necessary or reasonable for the complete answering, processing and / or justification in response to your message. Your message may also end up in our email archive, where it can be stored for 7 years as a result of backup procedures and legal retention obligations.

Participation in interactive areas of our websites

When you participate in interactive areas of our websites (such as a forum, membership site or electronic learning environment), for which you must register and log in, you are free to enter personal data in your profile, in your signature, in the messages that you post, and the like. This data is stored for an unlimited period, displayed in the relevant part of the website and made accessible by search engines. You make this information public yourself and this is your own responsibility. You are also free to use a fantasy name (pseudonym, 'nickname') when registering and not to enter any - or fictitious - personal data and to only post anonymous messages. The data posted by you will only be anonymised or deleted in exceptional cases,

Interviews en citaten

If, in our opinion, there is abuse or improper use of our facilities, we can deny access to certain IP numbers and / or e-mail addresses for a definite or indefinite period. We may make the relevant data available to these third parties in cases of abuse in which the interests of third parties are harmed. In the event of abuse, we may make attempts to trace the data to your person, for example to be able to contact you, a parent, guardian, teacher, supervisor, supervisor, ultimately responsible or the like, or to be able to file a report or to be able to sue.

Gt Kamot Trucks & Spare Parts makes data available when required by the authorities.

Send request to Gt Kamot Trucks & Spare Parts
